Game flow By screen Puzzle data Examples Checklist
Writer goal

Keep each screen short and clear. The player should always know: what is this, what do I do next, and what did I learn?

Player flow

Big picture
1 HomeWelcome the player and explain the loop in one short paragraph.
2 TutorialTeach Spin, Guess, Solve in three short steps.
3 GameShow category, difficulty, hint, letter board, and status messages.
4 SolveLet the player enter the full answer with the on-screen keyboard.
5 ResultReveal the term, explain it, and give one practical action tip.
6 EndCelebrate the score and invite replay.

Example rewrite set

Sample only

Term: DIVIDEND

HintA company profit share paid to investors
DefinitionA dividend is money a company pays to shareholders from its profits. It is one way investors can earn from owning shares, even before selling them.
TipIf you receive dividends, consider reinvesting them instead of spending them. Small payouts can grow faster when they buy more units or shares.

Term: BUDGET

HintA plan for where your money should go
DefinitionA budget is a simple plan for your income, spending, saving, and debt payments. It helps you decide before the month starts instead of guessing after money is gone.
TipTry sorting this month’s spending into needs, wants, and savings. You only need a notes app or spreadsheet to spot one easy improvement.

Writer review checklist

Before handoff
  • For each screen, the player knows what just happened and what to do next.
  • All puzzle term entries are uppercase, letters + spaces only, and not too long for mobile tiles.
  • Each hint helps without leaking the answer, and does not include its own emoji.
  • Each def explains the idea; each tip gives one clear action.
  • Examples fit the finance-learning context when amounts or local habits are mentioned.
  • The longest answer and longest result copy are checked on a narrow phone width around 320px.
